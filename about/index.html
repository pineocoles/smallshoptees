ABOUT

WHO WE ARE

Small Shop Tees is a Portland-based custom branded t-shirt service built specifically for small businesses. We're not a mass-market print shop — we're a dedicated service that treats every client like a real partner.

We understand the reality of running a small business. You don't have time to coordinate with vendors, manage inventory, or chase down orders. That's why we built a service that handles everything for you — from design to delivery to automatic restocking.

OUR MISSION

Custom branded shirts are one of the most powerful marketing tools a small business can have — they turn your customers and staff into walking advertisements, build community recognition, and create lasting brand loyalty.

Our mission is to make that accessible. No minimums that price you out, no complicated ordering systems, no corporate runaround. Just simple, reliable, high-quality shirt service for the businesses that make Portland what it is.

WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT

One dedicated account rep. One phone number. Direct communication — no call centers, no ticket systems. We know your brand, your style, and your inventory needs. When you're running low, we handle it before you even notice.

Every order ships on hangers, ready to display. Every shirt is 200+ GSM 100% cotton — the kind that holds up to real wear. Every design goes through quality review before printing.
