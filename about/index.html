THE FOUNDER

Small Shop Tees started because I kept seeing the same problem: Portland business owners wanted great-looking branded shirts, but the process was a nightmare — slow turnarounds, hidden fees, vendors who disappeared after the order.

I grew up in Portland. I know how hard it is to run a small business here. So I built a service that handles everything — design to delivery to automatic restocking — so you never have to think about it again.

Every client gets my personal number. You're not a ticket in a queue. You're a neighbor.
