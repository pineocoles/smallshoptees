PRICING Q&A
ARE THERE ANY HIDDEN FEES?
+
No. Your quote includes everything — design, production, delivery, and hangers. The price we quote is the price you pay. We don't add setup fees, shipping charges, or artwork fees after the fact.
WHAT IF I NEED A RUSH ORDER?
+
Rush orders are available depending on our current production schedule. Contact us directly and we'll let you know what's possible. Rush orders may carry an additional fee, which we'll disclose upfront in your quote.
DO VOLUME DISCOUNTS APPLY?
+
Yes — the more shirts per order, the lower the per-shirt price. Volume pricing tiers kick in at 50, 100, and 200+ shirts. Your quote will reflect the correct tier automatically based on your quantity.
WHAT IS THE PAYMENT PROCESS?
+
All orders are invoiced manually after your design is approved and before production begins. We do not process payments online — your account rep will send you an invoice directly. We currently accept bank transfer and major credit cards.
HOW IS RESTOCK PRICING DIFFERENT?
+
Enrolled restock clients receive a reduced per-shirt rate compared to standard first-time orders. This is because restocks use an existing approved design (no design facilitation needed) and follow a pre-set size breakdown, which reduces our time and cost. Those savings get passed to you.
