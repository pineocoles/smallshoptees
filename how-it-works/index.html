Here's exactly what happens after you contact us. No surprises, no runaround.
TYPICAL TIMELINE: 10–14 BUSINESS DAYS FROM DESIGN APPROVAL TO DELIVERY
01
DAY 1
YOU REACH OUT

Call, text, or submit our online quote form. Tell us roughly what you're thinking — even a vague idea is enough to get started. We respond within 1 business day.

USUALLY SAME DAY OR NEXT DAY
02
DAY 1–2
WE CONSULT ON DESIGN DIRECTION

We'll have a quick call or text conversation to understand your brand, colors, and vibe. No design experience required on your end — just describe what you're going for and we'll take it from there.

1–2 BUSINESS DAYS TO FIRST DRAFT
03
DAY 2–4
WE BUILD A MOCKUP & GET YOUR APPROVAL

Our design team creates a full mockup of your shirt. We send it to you for review. Revisions are unlimited and free — we keep adjusting until you're completely happy with it.

ORDER PLACED SAME DAY AS APPROVAL
04
DAY 4–5
WE PLACE THE ORDER WITH OUR SUPPLIER

Once you approve the design, we place the production order immediately. We work with trusted local suppliers to ensure consistent quality on every run.

10–14 BUSINESS DAYS PRODUCTION
05
DAY 14–19
WE DELIVER DIRECTLY TO YOUR BUSINESS

We bring the shirts to you — on hangers, ready to display or wear. No unpacking boxes, no assembly. We deliver directly to your business address anywhere in the Portland metro area. Free delivery on every order.

AUTOMATIC ENROLLMENT ON DELIVERY
06
ONGOING
WE ENROLL YOU IN THE RESTOCK PROGRAM

After your first order, we enroll you in our automatic restock program. We track your inventory level and initiate a reorder before you run out — so you never face a gap in stock. You can manage and view your inventory through your client portal, or simply contact your account rep directly.
