THE FULL PROCESS
01
DAY 1
YOU REACH OUT

Call, text, or submit our online quote form. Tell us roughly what you're thinking — even a vague idea is enough to get started. We respond within 1 business day.

02
DAY 1–2
WE CONSULT ON DESIGN DIRECTION

We'll have a quick call or text conversation to understand your brand, colors, and vibe. No design experience required on your end — just describe what you're going for and we'll take it from there.

03
DAY 2–4
WE BUILD A MOCKUP & GET YOUR APPROVAL

Our design team creates a full mockup of your shirt. We send it for review. Revisions are unlimited and free — we keep adjusting until you're completely happy with it.

04
DAY 4–5
WE PLACE THE ORDER WITH OUR SUPPLIER

Once you approve the design, we place the production order immediately. We work with trusted local suppliers to ensure consistent quality on every run.

05
DAY 14–19
WE DELIVER DIRECTLY TO YOUR BUSINESS

We bring the shirts to you — on hangers, ready to display or wear. No unpacking boxes, no assembly. Free delivery anywhere in the Portland metro area.

06
ONGOING
WE ENROLL YOU IN THE RESTOCK PROGRAM

After your first order, we enroll you in our automatic restock program. We track your inventory level and initiate a reorder before you run out — you never face a gap in stock. Manage everything through your client portal or contact your account rep directly.
