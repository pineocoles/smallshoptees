ABOUT US

WE'RE SMALL SHOP TEES

We started Small Shop Tees because we believe every small business deserves gear they're proud to wear — without the hassle, high minimums, or confusing process that big print shops put you through.

Based right here in Portland, Oregon, we work exclusively with small businesses. That means local restaurants, coffee shops, breweries, gyms, food trucks, nonprofits, and every other kind of small operation that makes Portland great.

We're a small team ourselves, so we genuinely get it. We keep things fast, honest, and easy.
