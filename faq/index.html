ORDERING & MINIMUMS
WHAT IS THE MINIMUM ORDER?
+
50 shirts per initial order. This minimum applies to your first order and allows us to offer competitive pricing per shirt. For restock orders from enrolled clients, the minimum may be lower and is determined at the time of enrollment based on your usage patterns.
HOW LONG DOES DELIVERY TAKE?
+
Typically 10–14 business days from design approval to delivery at your door. This includes production time and local Portland delivery. Rush orders may be available — contact us and we'll see what we can do. We'll always give you a realistic timeline upfront.
WHAT SIZES DO YOU OFFER?
+
We offer XS through 3XL on most styles. The exact size range depends on the shirt style — some fitted styles go up to 2XL. Your quote will confirm size availability for your selected style.
DESIGN
DO I NEED TO PROVIDE A DESIGN?
+
No — just an idea. You don't need design experience, artwork files, or even a logo to get started. Our design team creates your artwork from scratch based on your description. Unlimited revisions are included at no extra cost. If you do have a logo or some inspiration images, even better — send them over and we'll work from there.
HOW MANY REVISIONS DO I GET?
+
Unlimited. We keep revising until you're completely happy with the mockup. We want your shirts to look great, so we don't rush this step. Once you approve the design, we move straight to production.
CAN I CHANGE MY SHIRT STYLE LATER?
+
Yes — contact your account rep directly to update your product selection. Style changes require a new design consultation and production run, so they don't happen automatically through a restock. Your rep will walk you through the process.
DELIVERY & SERVICE
DO YOU SERVE AREAS OUTSIDE PORTLAND?
+
Currently we serve the Portland metro area only. This is intentional — our local, personal service model depends on proximity. We're expanding soon, but for now, Portland is the city we're focused on doing right. If you're just outside the metro, contact us and we'll let you know if we can accommodate you.
DO SHIRTS ARRIVE ON HANGERS?
+
Yes. Every order is delivered directly to your business on hangers, ready to display or wear. There's no unpacking, steaming, or prep work required on your end. This is part of our standard service — no extra charge.
WHAT IF I'M NOT HAPPY WITH MY ORDER?
+
We stand behind our work. If there's a quality issue or the shirts don't match what was approved, contact us within 5 business days of delivery. We'll make it right. We review each issue case by case and our priority is that you're satisfied with the result.
RESTOCK PROGRAM
WHAT DOES THE RESTOCK PROGRAM INCLUDE?
+
Once enrolled, we monitor your inventory level and automatically initiate a reorder before you run out. You don't need to track stock or remember to call us. You'll receive a notification when a restock order is triggered. Enrolled clients also receive a reduced per-shirt rate compared to standard orders.
HOW DO I ENROLL IN THE RESTOCK PROGRAM?
+
Enrollment happens automatically after your first order is delivered. Your account rep will confirm enrollment details at delivery, including the reorder threshold and your preferred size breakdown for future restocks. You can adjust these at any time by contacting your rep.
CLIENT ACCOUNT
HOW DO I SET UP A CLIENT ACCOUNT?
+
We create your client portal account after your first order is placed and delivered. You'll receive login credentials from your account rep. There's no self-registration — accounts are created manually to ensure every client gets personal onboarding.
WHAT CAN I DO IN THE CLIENT PORTAL?
+
The client portal lets you view your current inventory level, see your order history, place restock orders, and check estimated delivery dates. You can also view your account rep's contact info for direct communication.
