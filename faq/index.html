FAQ

Everything you'd ask us on a sales call — answered before you even pick up the phone.

ORDERING & MINIMUMS
WHAT'S THE MINIMUM ORDER?
+
50 shirts for a new design. This minimum applies to your first order and allows us to offer competitive pricing per shirt. For restock orders from enrolled clients, the minimum drops to 25 shirts and is determined at enrollment based on your usage patterns.
HOW LONG DOES IT TAKE?
+
10–14 business days from design approval to delivery for your first order. Restock orders typically arrive in 7–10 business days since the design is already approved. We'll always give you a realistic timeline upfront, and rush options may be available — just ask.
DESIGN
DO I NEED MY OWN DESIGN?
+
No — just an idea. You don't need design experience, artwork files, or even a logo to get started. Our team builds your design from scratch based on your description. Unlimited revisions are included at no extra cost. If you have a logo or inspiration images, even better.
HOW MANY REVISIONS DO I GET?
+
Unlimited. We keep revising until you're completely happy with the mockup. We want your shirts to look great, so we don't rush this step. Once you approve the design, we move straight to production.
PRODUCTS
WHAT SHIRTS DO YOU USE?
+
Our standard tee is 200+ GSM 100% cotton — a quality heavyweight fabric that holds up and looks great. Available in multiple colors including Black, White, Navy, Red, Forest Green, Gray, and Brown. Sizes XS through 3XL on most styles.
WHAT PRINTING METHODS DO YOU USE?
+
We offer both screen printing and DTG (Direct-to-Garment) printing depending on your design and order size. Screen printing is ideal for large runs with simple, bold designs. DTG works great for detailed artwork and smaller quantities. We'll recommend the best method for your specific order.
DELIVERY & SERVICE
WHAT AREAS DO YOU SERVE?
+
Currently we serve the Portland metro area only. This is intentional — our local, personal service model depends on proximity. We're expanding soon, but Portland is the city we're focused on doing right. If you're just outside the metro, contact us and we'll let you know if we can accommodate you.
DO SHIRTS ARRIVE ON HANGERS?
+
Yes. Every order is delivered directly to your business on hangers, ready to display or wear. There's no unpacking, steaming, or prep work required on your end. This is part of our standard service — no extra charge.
CAN I SEE SAMPLES BEFORE ORDERING?
+
Yes — contact us and we'll arrange a sample viewing. We can show you fabric quality and print examples so you know exactly what you're getting before committing to a full order.
PRICING
WHAT'S THE PRICING?
+
Pricing runs from approximately $15–$18 per shirt depending on quantity and whether you're enrolled in our restock program. Standard orders start at ~$18/shirt with a 50-shirt minimum. Restock program clients get ~$15/shirt with a 25-shirt minimum. Visit our Pricing page for full details, or request a free quote for your specific order.
ARE THERE HIDDEN FEES?
+
No. Your quote includes everything — design, production, delivery, and hangers. The price we quote is the price you pay. We don't add setup fees, shipping charges, or artwork fees after the fact.
RESTOCK PROGRAM
HOW DOES THE RESTOCK PROGRAM WORK?
+
After your first order is delivered, we enroll you automatically. We track your inventory level and initiate a reorder before you run out — so you never face a gap in stock. You'll receive a notification when a restock order is triggered, and you can adjust thresholds anytime by contacting your account rep.
HOW DO I ENROLL IN THE RESTOCK PROGRAM?
+
Enrollment happens automatically after your first order is delivered. Your account rep will confirm enrollment details at delivery, including the reorder threshold and your preferred size breakdown for future restocks. You can adjust these at any time by contacting your rep.
